Who We Are

VERTICAL
FROM THE
Start

ATLANTA'S VERTICAL
Drama Studio

Vertical Studios ATL was built with a singular focus: produce world-class narrative drama for the vertical screen. We're not a traditional studio that adapted — vertical is all we've ever done.

Based in Atlanta, Georgia, we work with writers, directors, and platforms who understand that the mobile-first audience deserves stories crafted specifically for how they watch — not reformatted from somewhere else.

Our experienced co-founders have collectively produced and directed over 50 vertical series for platforms across the industry, bringing unmatched format expertise to every project we take on.

What Drives Us

OUR
Values

01

FORMAT MASTERY

We don't adapt — we originate. Every script, shot, and cut is designed for 9:16 from day one. Vertical isn't a constraint; it's our creative language.

02

STORY FIRST

Short runtime doesn't mean shallow storytelling. We demand compelling characters, genuine tension, and satisfying narrative arcs in every episode we produce.

03

PLATFORM PARTNERSHIP

We build long-term relationships with platforms, not transactions. Understanding what each platform needs — technically and creatively — makes our content perform better.
