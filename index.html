Production

SERIES DEVELOPMENT

We develop original micro-drama IP from the ground up — scripts, formats, and episode structures purpose-designed for vertical platform consumption and mobile-native audiences.

Shot in 9:16 from day one. Our Atlanta-based crews know how to compose, light, and direct for the vertical frame — no cropping, no compromise, no afterthought.
